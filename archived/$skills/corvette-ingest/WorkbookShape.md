Workbook Shape

The workbook is a 23-sheet 2027 Chevrolet Corvette pricing/configuration guide. All sheets are visible, with no formal tables, pivots, or embedded charts detected.

The sheet groups follow a consistent model-family pattern: 1 = Stingray, 2 = Grand Sport, 3 = Z06, and 4 = ZR1 / ZR1X.

Most configuration sheets use row 1 as the model-family banner, row 2 as the availability legend, row 3 as the column header, columns A–C for RPO code/reference/description, and columns D onward for model/trim availability.

Notable Details

Most model-family sheets are single-table matrices rather than multi-tabular reports; segmentation is primarily by sheet name and row 1 model banner.

Equipment Groups sheets are the clearest multi-section sheets, with merged gray section bars for Equipment Groups, Additional Options, and Regulatory Options.

Color and Trim sheets use extensive merged headers and note rows, unlike the RPO availability sheets.

Price Schedule is the only sheet organized as a pricing document with many named option-category subsections and bottom disclaimer rows.
